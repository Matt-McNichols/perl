Traditional Addition

  • Here is a step by step procedure that works every time
  • students are not encouraged to think about the physical understanding
  • Students easily lose context with the problem

Creative Addition

  • The students are not constrained to following and algorithm
  • Students are free to get as creative as they would like
  • Students who design their own method for addition understand the concept more
